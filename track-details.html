Track Details

Track 1: Smart Systems and Applications

Focuses on the design, development, and deployment of smart systems in various domains.
  • Smart cities and infrastructure
  • Smart homes and buildings
  • Smart healthcare systems
  • Internet of Things (IoT) solutions for intelligent environments
  • Smart transportation and logistics
  • Automation in agriculture and smart farming

Track 2: Intelligent Computing and Data Analytics

Explores the role of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science in solving complex computational problems.
  • Machine learning and deep learning algorithms
  • Data mining and predictive analytics
  • Big data technologies and applications
  • Natural language processing and sentiment analysis
  • Reinforcement learning and adaptive systems
  • Cognitive computing and knowledge representation

Track 3: Advanced Networking and Communication Technologies

Covers emerging technologies in communication networks and their integration with intelligent systems.
  • 5G/6G networks and beyond
  • Wireless sensor networks and edge computing
  • Blockchain and distributed systems in smart networks
  • Network security and privacy solutions
  • Cloud computing and virtualization in intelligent systems
  • Cyber-physical systems and real-time communication

Track 4: Sustainable and Secure Computing

Focuses on ethical, sustainable, and secure applications of smart technologies.
  • Green computing and energy-efficient algorithms
  • Sustainable technology solutions for industries
  • Cybersecurity challenges and solutions for intelligent systems
  • Blockchain for secure and transparent systems
  • Ethical AI and responsible technology use
  • Data privacy and protection in smart technologies
