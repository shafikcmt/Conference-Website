About INCSTIC-2025

Welcome to INCSTIC-2025: 1st International Conference on Smart Technologies and Intelligent Computing will be organized by School of Computer Science and Engineering, during 5th- 6th  May 2025 at Geeta University, Campus – Panipat, Haryana, India.

The conference is an annual global event designed to bring together researchers, academicians, industry leaders, and technology enthusiasts from around the world. This conference serves as a dynamic platform for presenting and discussing groundbreaking innovations, cutting-edge research, and transformative ideas in the rapidly evolving fields of smart technologies and intelligent computing.

With the increasing integration of intelligence into systems and the proliferation of smart devices, INCSTIC seeks to address the challenges and opportunities presented by this technological revolution. The conference emphasizes the critical role of smart technologies and intelligent computing in shaping the future of industries, societies, and economies worldwide. INCSTIC aims to foster collaboration among researchers, academia, industry to bridge the gap between research and real-world applications. Participants will gain insights into the latest advancements in smart cities, intelligent systems, advanced networking solutions, data analytics, and other transformative domains. The conference also serves as a breeding ground for novel ideas, interdisciplinary research, and innovative applications that address contemporary challenges, such as sustainability, cybersecurity, and user-centric design.

Pedagogy

All accepted and presented papers at the conference will be published in the AIP Proceedings and indexed in the Scopus repository, subject to final approval by the AIP.

The idea of the conference is to bring the scholars, scientists, industrialists from all over the world on a common platform with the following objectives:

  • To provide a platform for presenting cutting-edge research in smart technologies and intelligent computing.
  • To explore the integration of intelligent systems in various domains, including engineering, healthcare, education, and business.
  • To facilitate collaboration between academia and industry for impactful innovations.
  • To promote interdisciplinary research and address societal challenges through smart technological solutions.

Vision and Goals

To establish the International Conference on Smart Technologies and Intelligent Computing (InCSTIC) as a global leader in advancing knowledge, fostering innovation, contributing to a smarter, more sustainable, and interconnected world. Key goals of the conference include:

  • Advance Research and Innovation Foster the presentation and exchange of ground breaking research and innovative solutions in smart technologies and intelligent computing.

About GTH

Geeta Technical Hub (GTH) is the training division of Geeta University, dedicated to equipping students with industry-relevant skills. We offer a variety of certification programs, hands-on projects, internships, and placement opportunities that bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world corporate needs. Our programs cover multiple technologies, including Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, and more. Students gain practical experience through live projects and internships with top-tier companies, preparing them for successful careers in the tech industry. In addition to technical expertise, GTH emphasizes soft skills like communication and problem-solving, ensuring well-rounded development. Our strong industry partnerships provide exclusive placement opportunities, helping students step confidently into the job market. At GTH, we’re committed to shaping the future of technology by fostering innovation, professionalism, and excellence in every student.
